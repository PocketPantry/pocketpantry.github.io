About Us

We are a team of people dedicated to serving the food-loving community.

Aleeza Ladhani
CEO

Aleeza Ladhani is a natural born foodie. As the CEO of Pocket Pantry, Aleeza's goal is to share her passion, skills, and expertise in the kitchen and business fields with fellow aspiring chefs in the community.

Atinderpaul Kanwar
CTO

The simplicity, usability, and friendliness of Pocket Pantry is all thanks to our CTO, Atinderpaul Kanwar. Atinderpaul has devised long-term plans for Pocket Pantry's future growth.

Ebrahim Khan
Marketing Manager

When Ebrahim isn't promoting Pocket Pantry's services or researching marketing strategies, he can be found in the gym pumping iron. Receiving his Bachelor's in Business Administration from Algonquin College, Ebrahim is an asset to our team's continued growth.

Joshua Kayoda
Graphic Designer

The image you create in your mind when you think of Pocket Pantry is the image of Joshua Kayoda's design work. With a degree in Integrated Media from OCAD University, Joshua has the ability to tell tales through photography, as he does in his position as Graphic Designer.
